# Code of Conduct

## Our Goal

InboxBridge should be a respectful, constructive, and welcoming project for users and contributors of different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

Please:

- be respectful and patient
- assume good intent until there is clear reason not to
- give feedback that is specific and constructive
- focus discussion on the work, not the person
- be open to correction and clarification
- help keep the project approachable for both technical and less technical users

## Unacceptable Behavior

This project does not accept:

- harassment, threats, or intimidation
- hateful, discriminatory, or demeaning language
- personal attacks or sustained hostility
- deliberate disruption of discussions, reviews, or issue threads
- doxxing or publication of private information
- knowingly misleading security, operational, or licensing claims

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content, and may limit participation when behavior harms the project or its community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the repository owner privately through GitHub.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to issues, pull requests, code review, discussions, and other project spaces.
